Web Servers and the Modern Internet

Introduction

Web servers are the backbone of the modern Internet. They deliver websites, applications, and services to billions of users worldwide. From the first web server created at CERN in 1990 to modern cloud-based infrastructure, web servers have evolved dramatically in performance, scalability, and security.
